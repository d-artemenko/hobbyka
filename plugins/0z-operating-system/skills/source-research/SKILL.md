# Source Research

## Workflow

1. Read `research/README.md`, `research/INDEX.md`, and `research/SOURCES.md`.
2. Register new sources in `research/SOURCES.md` before relying on them.
3. Put durable extracts under `research/extracts/`.
4. Put quick lookup pages under `research/indexes/`.
5. Prefer primary sources and local files when they exist.
6. Keep personal data and secrets out of extracts.
7. Update `research/INDEX.md` after adding useful material.

## Source Record

Each source entry should include:

- identifier;
- title or short name;
- location;
- status: `new`, `checked`, `extracted`, `stale`, or `rejected`;
- reason it matters;
- extract path when one exists.

## Boundary

Do not turn research into a hidden database. The durable form is Markdown plus
git.
